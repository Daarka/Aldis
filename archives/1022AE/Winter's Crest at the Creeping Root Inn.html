Cairn, along with everything she is wearing or carrying, is transformed into a misty cloud for 10 minutes or until she chooses to end the effect as an action. Or until she drops to 0 HP. She can pass through small holes, narrow openings, and even mere cracks, though she treats liquids as if they were solid surfaces. She can't fall and remains hovering in the air even if stunned or otherwise incapacitated. She cannot talk or manipulate objects, and anything she had been wearing or carrying cannot be used, dropped, or otherwise interacted with. She cannot attack or cast spells. If this effect ends within 1 minute of drinking the potions, she will continue to hover 1 ft off the floor with a flying speed of 10 ft for the remainder of that minute. Regardless of if the effect ends early or not, when it ends, Cairn will rematerialize in a rush of air, forcing all Large or smaller creatures within 30 ft of her to make a DC 15 STR saving throw or be pushed 10 ft away from her and knocked prone.
